The Mind Behind Voxyntra

A journey shaped by discipline, passion, and a lifelong love for music.

  • A Life in Rhythm. Born on July 1, 1963, in Malaysia, the journey began long before Voxyntra existed.Raised and educated in Penang up to pre-university level, life took a disciplined path — serving 36 years as a police officer. A career built on responsibility, structure, and dedication. Widely known as Musyu99, the name reflects a personal identity built over time — one that now continues through music and creation. Yet beneath that structure, music was always present. Not as a profession, but as a calling.
  • Driven by Passion, Not Formal Training. Without formal music education, everything was learned through passion, curiosity, and years of listening. Over time, the ability to play multiple instruments developed — keyboard, bass, guitar, and drums. There were moments of collaboration, performing alongside local musicians, exploring sound beyond personal boundaries. Music was never just a hobby. It was always a part of life.
  • Influenced by Timeless Sound. Growing up with music from the 1970s and beyond, the sound was shaped by classic rhythms and enduring styles. Hustle, Latin, Rock and Roll, R&B, Rock, Waltz, etc. These influences continue to live within every composition — now reimagined through modern technology.
  • From Passion to Creation. Over the years, nearly 100 original songs have been written. In November 2025, a new chapter began — exploring the world of AI music. Since then, more than 300 AI-generated tracks have been created, blending experience, instinct, and technology into a new form of expression.

A space for individuals who share the same passion — to explore, create, and push the boundaries of AI music together.

At the same time, this platform exists to give meaning to every creation.
