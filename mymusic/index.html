ABOUT MUSYU99

Known as Musyu99, a passionate music creator with over 400 original and AI-generated tracks, blending experience, instinct, and modern technology into a unique sound.

Driven by a deep love for all styles of music, with a special focus on the timeless grooves of the 60s to 80s era, Musyu99 aims to bring back the richness, soul, and musical brilliance that defined those golden years.

Every track created is more than just sound —
it is a revival of feeling, rhythm, and authenticity.

Through Voxyntra, the mission is simple:
to share music openly and creatively with everyone.

Whether you want to:

sing along through karaoke,
lay down your own bassline,
build your rhythm on drums,
or add your personal musical touch,

you are invited to become part of the music.

This is not just about listening.
This is about creating, feeling, and playing together.

Step into the sound.
Explore. Play. Create.
